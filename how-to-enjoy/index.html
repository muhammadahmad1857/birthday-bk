HOW TO ENJOY THIS

Bakar Birthday Experience

Follow these steps for the full cinematic + interactive flow.

Step 1: Enter Experience

Tap the ◈ Enter Experience button (top-left). The camera flies through a balloon world into the birthday room. Do not skip.

Step 2: Blow Candles (Experience Mode)

Use your mic and blow into it, or tap Blow Candle. Blow all 5 candles to complete the experience phase.

Step 3: Spin and Tap the 3D Cake

On the main page cake section, drag to rotate the cake and tap directly on flame tips. Blow all 6 candles to unlock cutting.

Step 4: Cut the Cake

After all flames are out, press CUT THE CAKE. Watch the animation and celebration sequence.

Step 5: Music Player

Use the bottom-right music player for playlist, visualizer, shuffle, and repeat.

Step 6: Scroll Full Story

Explore Hero, Age Stats, Cake, Wishes, Profile, and Ending sections. Stay for post-credits after cake cut.
