INTERACTIVE GUIDE

HOW TO ENJOY THIS

a field manual for the birthday experience

◈
ENTER EXPERIENCE

Hit the ◈ Enter Experience button (top-left). The camera flies through a world of balloons straight into the birthday room. Full cinematic. Don't skip it.

🕯
BLOW THE CANDLES

Inside the experience: actually blow into your mic and the candles go out one by one. Or tap the button if you're shy. Blow all 5 to unlock the finale.

🎂
SPIN & CLICK THE CAKE

The 3D cake on this page is draggable — spin it. Click directly on a flame to blow that candle out. All 6 must go before you can cut.

🔪
CUT THE CAKE

After all 6 candles are blown: a big CUT button appears. Press it. Something happens. Trust the process. Stay for the post-credits.

🎵
MUSIC PLAYER

Bottom-right corner — tap the 🎵 icon to open the player. Bakar's playlist. Visualizer included. Shuffle. Repeat. Vibe.

↓
SCROLL EVERYTHING

There are 6 sections: hero → age stats → cake → wishes → character profile → ending. Don't just sit on the first screen.
